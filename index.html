R24 Internal Marks Rules

Term Exams (20 marks):

  • Scale each mid (30→20): (mid/30)*20
  • Best mid * 75% + Other mid * 25%

AAT (15 marks):

  • Average AAT = (AAT1 + AAT2)/2
  • Scale: (avg/10)*15

Attendance (5 marks):

  • ≥90%: 5, ≥85%: 4, ≥80%: 3, ≥75%: 2, <75%: 0

Pass Criteria:

  • Total ≥16/40 AND Attendance ≥75%
